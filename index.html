About Us

The Person Behind The Work

Mitchell Jacques founded Engineered Water Solutions after more than three decades in mechanical and water engineering — from remote PNG islands to 60-metre tunnels beneath the Brisbane River.

As founder and director of Meyjor Industries (2012–2023), Mitchell grew the business from $1M to $12M in a single year while maintaining zero OH&S incidents across every site he's ever run. EWS continues that standard.

Mitchell brings not just engineering depth but commercial and legal rigour — holding a Bachelor of Business (QUT), Bachelor of Arts in Economics (UQ), Master of Business Communication (UoW), and a near-complete Juris Doctor in Commercial Law (ANU, Distinction average). When he signs off on a contract, he understands every word of it.
